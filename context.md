# Context for Content Generation

## About The Gyaan Project (TGP)
The Gyaan Project (formerly Audiogyan) is a podcast and YouTube channel dedicated to exploring creative wisdom. Since 2016, the platform has been chronicling ideas, philosophies, and stories of Indian luminaries. TGP serves as a contemporary creative archive—bridging the past with the present to inspire the future.
Hosted by Kedar Nimkar, the show features deep and wide-ranging conversations with designers, artists, musicians, writers, and thinkers. It also offers short-form content to help contextualize broader ideas in design and art.

Highlights
A labor of love since 2016, with over 320 episodes
Documented icons like B.V. Doshi, Sudhir Patwardhan, Sujata Keshavan, Ramu Ramanathan, Rajat Kapoor, Varun Grover, and many more
A thriving community of 5,000+ creatives and 8 million+ views
Ranked among India’s top 10 podcasts in Design and Art, with a 4.5-star rating across 90+ reviews

Mission
To archive and amplify the voices of South Asian designers, artists, writers, musicians, thinkers, philosophers, and other creative minds.

Vision
To encourage a deeper understanding of design and art, and their impact on culture, climate, and individual lives.


## About the Host (Kedar)
Kedar Nimkar is the host, curator, and producer of The Gyaan Project. A self-taught designer with 18+ years of experience crafting digital experiences, Kedar has previously led design teams at Jupiter, BookMyShow, Cleartrip, and Webchutney. Currently, he served as a Director of Design at PropertyGuru in Singapore. Kedar hails from Mumbai, India.
